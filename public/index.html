AR Hand Gesture Capture for Interactive Data Analytics

Thank you for agreeing to participate in the study for Hand Gesture Capture for Interactive Data Analytics. The aim of the study is to find out what are the intuitive hand gestures that people would default to when interacting in AR with data.

For the entire experiment, you will be given a list of data interactions and be required to record hand gesture that you think would achieve such task.
The duration of the study is approximately 30 minutes.

Please make sure you have read and understood the Explanatory Statement and signed the Consent Form before proceeding, both of which should have been provided to your prior to this study.
