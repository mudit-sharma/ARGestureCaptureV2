Instructions

  • Enter your Participant ID and click START to start recording gestures.
  • After the page loads, you'll have different interaction to perform mentioned on TOP of page with its discription.
  • Given interaction will be highilighted and you can decide how to perform the same with hand gestures.
  • You can use ONE or BOTH hands for interactions.
  • When you are ready, click Record to start recording the hand gestures.

  • If you dont like the current gesture, you can click RETRY to change it.
  • You can record different gestures for same interaction if you have more than one idea.
